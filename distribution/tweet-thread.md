## 3/

The international policy direction has settled:

– US established a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve (March 2025)
– Sweden's opposition filed a parliamentary motion (October 2024)
– Czech central-bank governor publicly considered allocation (January 2025)
– Switzerland's grassroots referendum failed (May 2026 — top-down works; bottom-up doesn't)
– The ECB is silent because the question is outside its mandate

Finland has not yet defined its position.

## 4/

The proposal is three sequential tracks:

A. Establish a published governance framework for state-held digital assets. Finland already custodies seized BTC across 3+ agencies with no framework. Track A governs that operational reality. Zero new monetary policy.

B. Establish the legal mechanism for permanent accumulation. A one-time amendment to the Confiscation Act. Mirrors the US framework. Budget-neutral.

C. Optional 0.5% of FX reserves allocated over 5 years. Debated openly in Eduskunta. Sized so even a 90% drawdown is &lt; 0.05% of total reserves.
